A Neighborhood Walkabout is a set of pictures of new and exciting places in your neighborhood that you have to find. Neighborhood Walkabouts are a good way to get outside while having fun. They were created by Ben Goldberg, Jocelyn Glazier, and Steve Goldberg in response to the 2019/2020 COVID-19 outbreak in an attempt to help get people outside. People create Neighborhood Walkabouts and send them to an email address, where we add them to the crowdsourced map. You can create your own using the "Create A Walkabout" tab above. Neighborhood Walkabouts are totally free and the code for this website is open source. We hope you have fun walking about your neighborhood! 🌳
